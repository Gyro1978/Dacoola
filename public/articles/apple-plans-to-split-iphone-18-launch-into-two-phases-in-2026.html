Apple plans to split iPhone 18 launch into two phases in 2026

Apple is reportedly planning to split the iPhone 18 launch into two phases in 2026, according to supply chain sources cited by The Information. The company will release the Pro versions in fall 2026, followed by the standard and more affordable iPhone 18 models roughly half a year later.

Why It Matters

This staggered release strategy could help Apple maximize sales momentum by targeting high-end buyers first, then capturing budget-conscious consumers later. It also reflects a growing industry trend of segmenting product launches to maintain market buzz and manage supply chain constraints. If successful, this approach may become a blueprint for future iPhone releases.

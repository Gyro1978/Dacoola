xMEMS Brings Micro Cooling Fan-on-a-Chip to AI Data Centers

xMEMS Labs, a leader in MEMS-based chip innovation, has announced the expansion of its µCooling fan-on-a-chip technology to AI data centers. This advancement aims to improve thermal management for high-performance AI systems using compact, efficient AI cooling tech.

The micro-scale cooling solution could revolutionize heat dissipation in densely packed server environments. By integrating fan functionality directly into chips, xMEMS reduces energy consumption while maintaining cooling effectiveness.

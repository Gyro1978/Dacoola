Proving Grounds: Governments Build Sandboxes to Test AI

Governments worldwide are increasingly turning to AI testing sandboxes as a way to evaluate artificial intelligence systems in controlled, real-world-like environments. These sandboxes allow policymakers and developers to assess AI performance, risks, and regulatory compliance before full-scale deployment. The approach aims to balance innovation with safety, ensuring AI technologies meet ethical and legal standards.

These testing environments simulate various scenarios where AI systems might be applied, from public services to law enforcement. By isolating AI models in sandboxes, governments can identify potential biases, security vulnerabilities, and unintended consequences without exposing critical infrastructure or citizens to undue risk.

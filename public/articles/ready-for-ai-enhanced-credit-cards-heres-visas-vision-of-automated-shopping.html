Ready for AI-enhanced credit cards? Here's Visa's vision of automated shopping

Visa has unveiled a vision for AI-enhanced credit cards that could automate shopping by using AI agents to find and order products on behalf of users. While details remain sparse, the announcement suggests a future where AI handles routine purchases, reducing manual effort for consumers.

Why It Matters

This move signals a growing trend of integrating AI into financial services to streamline everyday tasks. If successful, AI-enhanced credit cards could redefine convenience in e-commerce, potentially reducing decision fatigue and saving time for users. However, questions around security, privacy, and user control will need addressing before widespread adoption.

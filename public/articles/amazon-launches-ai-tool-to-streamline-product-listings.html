Amazon Launches AI Tool to Streamline Product Listings

Amazon has introduced a new generative AI-powered tool called Enhance My Listing to assist sellers in refining product details. The tool automatically suggests titles, descriptions, and missing attributes based on seasonal trends, reducing the manual effort required for updates. Currently rolling out to select U.S. sellers, it builds on Amazon’s existing AI features, which have already been used by over 900,000 merchants.

How the Amazon AI Tool Works

The tool leverages Amazon Bedrock, the company’s generative AI service, to analyze customer engagement data and generate relevant listing improvements. Sellers can review, modify, or reject these suggestions before applying changes to their product pages. Amazon reports that 90% of AI-generated content is accepted without edits, though this doesn’t necessarily confirm accuracy-some sellers may not scrutinize the outputs thoroughly.

This follows earlier AI integrations, such as a 2023 feature for writing product descriptions and a March 2024 update allowing sellers to create listings from website URLs or minimal inputs. The latest tool aims to simplify catalog management, particularly for merchants with extensive inventories.

Market Context and Competition

Amazon isn’t alone in applying generative AI to e-commerce. Google, eBay, Meta, and Shopify have released similar tools, while startups like Avataar and Superscale focus on AI-generated marketing assets. The trend reflects broader industry efforts to automate repetitive tasks and enhance seller efficiency.

Pros & Cons

Pros
  • Reduces manual workload for sellers managing large inventories.
  • Uses real-time data to align listings with seasonal trends.
Cons
  • Potential inaccuracies if sellers don’t review AI suggestions carefully.
  • Limited initial availability, with a phased rollout in the U.S.

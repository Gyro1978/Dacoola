Visa and Mastercard Roll Out AI-Powered Shopping Solutions

Visa and Mastercard are stepping into the AI-powered shopping space with new innovations. Visa’s “Intelligent Commerce” platform allows AI agents to shop and buy products on behalf of users, guided by preselected preferences.

The move signals a shift toward automation in consumer spending, with AI handling transactions seamlessly. Mastercard is expected to announce similar AI-driven shopping capabilities soon.

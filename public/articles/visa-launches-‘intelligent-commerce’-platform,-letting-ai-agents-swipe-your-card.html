Visa launches ‘Intelligent Commerce’ platform, letting AI agents swipe your card—safely, it says

Visa has introduced its Intelligent Commerce platform, allowing AI assistants to make secure purchases using a user's credit card. The system offers personalized automation while letting consumers set spending limits, aiming to streamline online shopping. This AI commerce platform promises enhanced convenience without compromising security.

Why It Matters

Visa’s move signals a shift toward AI-driven financial autonomy, where trusted agents handle transactions on behalf of users. By integrating spending controls, the platform addresses security concerns while tapping into the growing demand for automated commerce. If successful, this could redefine how consumers interact with digital payments, reducing friction in everyday purchases.

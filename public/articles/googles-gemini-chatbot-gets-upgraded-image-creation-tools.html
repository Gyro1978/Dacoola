Google’s Gemini chatbot gets upgraded image creation tools

Published on April 30, 2025 by AI News Team   |   View Original Source

Google’s Gemini Chatbot Enhances Image Editing Capabilities

Google announced a major Gemini chatbot upgrade, introducing native image editing for both AI-generated and user-uploaded photos. The feature begins rolling out today and will expand to most countries and over 45 languages in the coming weeks.

The update allows users to modify images directly within the Gemini app, streamlining creative workflows. Google’s blog post highlights this as a step toward more versatile AI-powered tools.

IBM CEO urges the Trump Administration to increase — not cut — federal AI R&D funding

Published on May 06, 2025 by AI News Team   |   View Original Source
<h2>IBM CEO Urges the Trump Administration to Increase — Not Cut — Federal AI R&amp;D Funding</h2> <p>IBM CEO Arvind Krishna has joined other tech leaders in advocating for increased federal funding for AI R&amp;D, opposing potential cuts. Krishna emphasized his strong personal and corporate stance, stating, “We have been an extremely strong advocate... that federally funded R&amp;D should be increased.” His comments highlight the tech industry’s push for sustained government investment in AI and related technologies. </p> <h3>Why It Matters</h3> <p>The call for increased AI R&amp;D funding reflects the growing recognition of AI as a strategic priority for national competitiveness. Federal investment accelerates breakthroughs, supports workforce development, and ensures the U.S. maintains leadership in AI innovation. Krishna’s stance signals broader industry concerns that budget cuts could stifle progress, leaving the U.S. behind in the global AI race.</p>

Google AI Course Sets World Record with 280,000 Signups

Google and Kaggle’s GenAI Intensive course made history by attracting over 280,000 signups in just 20 days, earning a Guinness World Record for the largest virtual AI conference attendance in one week. The five-day Google AI course provided a mix of theory, hands-on labs, and expert-led discussions on generative AI, from foundational models to real-world applications.

Key Features of the GenAI Intensive Course

The course was structured to balance learning formats, including podcasts, livestreams, and interactive labs. Each day focused on a specific topic: foundational models, embeddings, AI agents, domain-specific LLMs, and MLOps for generative AI. Participants worked with tools like the Gemini API, Vertex AI, and LangGraph through Kaggle’s code labs. A capstone project allowed learners to apply their knowledge in a practical setting.

Community engagement was a major component, with live Q&A sessions featuring Google experts and moderated discussions on Discord. For those who missed the live sessions, a self-paced version is now available on Kaggle.

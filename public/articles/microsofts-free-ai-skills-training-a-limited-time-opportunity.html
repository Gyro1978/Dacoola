Microsoft's Free AI Skills Training: A Limited-Time Opportunity

Microsoft's AI Skills Fest is offering free AI skills training until May 28, providing courses for beginners and advanced learners alike. The program includes self-paced lessons, hackathons, and certification discounts, making it a valuable opportunity for anyone looking to expand their AI knowledge.

Key Features of Microsoft’s AI Skills Fest

The AI Skills Fest covers a broad range of topics, from introductory lessons like Using AI for Everyday Tasks to advanced courses such as Microsoft Fabric for data analytics. The training is available in multiple languages, ensuring accessibility for a global audience. While the core lessons are free, certification exams require additional fees-though participants can enter a sweepstakes for free vouchers.

GitHub is also offering a 50% discount on its GitHub Copilot certification exam for those who complete the related module. The discount is valid until May 28, 2025, with exams required by June 30, 2025.

Hands-On Learning Experience

Participants can engage in interactive sessions, including a Minecraft Education module designed for younger learners and a GitHub Copilot tutorial for developers. The LinkedIn Learning course, AI for Organizational Leaders, provides insights into AI’s business applications and ethical considerations.

Microsoft recently set a Guinness World Record with 126,151 participants completing an AI lesson in 24 hours, surpassing the previous record of 46,045. This achievement highlights the growing interest in AI skills training.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Microsoft’s AI Skills Fest completely free?

Yes, the training courses are free, but certification exams require separate fees.

Can I access the courses after May 28?

Some lessons remain available, but the full Skills Fest benefits expire after the deadline.

What languages are supported?

Courses are available in over 30 languages, including English, Spanish, and Mandarin.

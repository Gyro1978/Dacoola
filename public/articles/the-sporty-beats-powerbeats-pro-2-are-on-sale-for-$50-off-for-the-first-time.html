The sporty Beats Powerbeats Pro 2 are on sale for $50 off for the first time

The Beats Powerbeats Pro 2, known for their fitness-friendly design and active noise cancellation, are now available at an all-time low price of $199.95 ($50 off) at Amazon, Walmart, and B&H Photo. These wireless earbuds feature improved ear hooks, IPX4 water resistance, and heart rate monitoring (though limited for iOS users). While they don’t match the AirPods Pro or Sony XM5 in sound quality, they deliver strong bass and solid performance for workouts.

Why It Matters

This discount marks the first significant price drop for the Powerbeats Pro 2 since their February release, making them a more compelling choice for fitness enthusiasts. The inclusion of ANC and transparency mode—absent in the first-gen model—enhances their appeal, though the heart rate monitoring limitation for iPhone users is a notable drawback. For Android users or those prioritizing durability and gym-friendly features over audiophile-grade sound, this Beats earbuds sale presents a solid opportunity to upgrade.

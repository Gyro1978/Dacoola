OpenAI reverses course, says its nonprofit will remain in control of its business operations

OpenAI has reversed its earlier decision to convert to a for-profit organization, announcing that its nonprofit division will retain control over its business operations. The company stated that its for-profit wing, which has been under the nonprofit since 2019, will transition into a public benefit corporation (PBC). This move ensures the nonprofit maintains oversight, marking a significant OpenAI governance change.

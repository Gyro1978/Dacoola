Your Service Teams Just Got a New Coworker — and It’s a 15B-Parameter Super Genius Built by ServiceNow and NVIDIA

ServiceNow and NVIDIA have introduced Apriel Nemotron 15B, a compact yet powerful enterprise AI model designed for real-time reasoning, complex workflows, and scalability in IT, HR, and customer service. Developed using NVIDIA NeMo and trained on NVIDIA DGX Cloud, the model prioritizes speed, efficiency, and accuracy while reducing inference costs. It leverages ServiceNow’s domain-specific data and NVIDIA’s open dataset to enhance enterprise AI agents.

Why It Matters

This collaboration marks a shift toward specialized AI models that balance performance and cost, addressing enterprise needs more effectively than general-purpose LLMs. By integrating real-time workflow data and continuous learning, Apriel Nemotron 15B could significantly boost productivity—demonstrated in AstraZeneca’s case, where it reclaimed 90,000 employee hours. The partnership highlights the growing demand for AI solutions that evolve with business demands, setting a benchmark for future enterprise AI deployments.

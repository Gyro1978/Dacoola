A Gears of War Remaster Is Coming to PlayStation 5, PC and Xbox This Summer

The Gears of War remaster is expanding beyond its original Xbox exclusivity, arriving on PlayStation 5, PC, and Xbox this summer. The iconic shooter, once a Microsoft-only title, will now let PlayStation players experience its intense, chaotic world for the first time.

Why It Matters

This move signals a shift in platform exclusivity, as Microsoft-owned franchises like Gears of War increasingly appear on rival consoles. By bringing the remaster to PlayStation 5, Microsoft may be testing broader cross-platform strategies, potentially opening doors for more Xbox exclusives to follow. For fans, it means greater accessibility—whether they’re longtime Xbox players or newcomers on PlayStation.

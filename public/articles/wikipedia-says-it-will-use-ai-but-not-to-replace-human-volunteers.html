Wikipedia says it will use AI, but not to replace human volunteers

Published on April 30, 2025 by AI News Team

Wikipedia’s AI strategy prioritizes human editors over automation

The Wikimedia Foundation unveiled its three-year AI strategy, emphasizing that artificial intelligence won’t replace Wikipedia’s volunteer editors. Instead, the Wikipedia AI strategy will focus on developing tools to streamline workflows and remove technical barriers for moderators and contributors.

AI as an assistant, not a replacement

New AI-powered features will help editors and patrollers work more efficiently, maintaining Wikipedia’s community-driven model. The foundation clarified that human oversight remains central to content accuracy and integrity.

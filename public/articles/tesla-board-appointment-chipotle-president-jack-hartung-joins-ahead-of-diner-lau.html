<h2>Tesla Board Appointment: Chipotle President Jack Hartung Joins Ahead of Diner Launch</h2> <p>Tesla has appointed Jack Hartung, Chipotle’s longtime president and former CFO, to its board of directors, according to a recent SEC filing. The move comes as Tesla finalizes plans for its 1950s-style diner and charging station in Los Angeles. Hartung brings decades of restaurant industry expertise, having led Chipotle’s financial and operational growth to over 3,700 locations globally. </p> <!-- DACCOOLA_IN_ARTICLE_AD_HERE --> <h3>A Strategic Addition to Tesla’s Board</h3> <p>Hartung’s appointment signals Tesla’s focus on blending its electric vehicle infrastructure with hospitality. His background at Chipotle and McDonald’s-where he spent nearly 20 years-positions him to advise Tesla as it ventures into food service with its retro-futuristic diner. Last year, Tesla posted a job listing for a "Tesla Diner Experience Specialist," hinting at ambitions beyond charging stations. </p> <p>The timing of Hartung’s arrival suggests Tesla may leverage his food industry connections to refine its diner concept. While his financial acumen is transferable, his network could prove invaluable as Tesla expands its customer experience offerings. </p> <h4>Hartung’s Industry Credentials</h4> <p>Hartung oversaw Chipotle’s finance, supply chain, and safety strategies during its rapid expansion. Before Chipotle, he served as VP and CFO of McDonald’s partner brands group. His waiver of board compensation aligns with Tesla’s existing director practices, though his son-in-law’s employment as a Tesla technician was disclosed as a related-party transaction.</p>

A stealth AI model beat DALL-E and Midjourney on a popular benchmark. Its creator just landed $30M.

Recraft, a San Francisco-based startup, has raised a $30 million Series B round led by Accel after its AI model outperformed OpenAI’s DALL-E and Midjourney in a respected industry benchmark last year. The funding round also included investments from Khosla Ventures and Madrona. This follows Recraft’s earlier $12 million raise, signaling strong investor confidence in its AI model benchmark performance.

Why It Matters

Recraft’s success highlights the growing competition in generative AI, where smaller players can challenge industry giants like OpenAI. The $30M funding injection suggests investors see potential in specialized AI models that outperform established ones in key benchmarks. This could accelerate innovation in AI-generated imagery, pushing incumbents to improve their offerings while opening doors for niche-focused startups.

Control the Composition of AI-Generated Images With the NVIDIA AI Blueprint for 3D-Guided Generative AI

AI-powered image generation has evolved rapidly, but achieving fine-tuned creative control remains a challenge. NVIDIA’s new AI image generation control solution, the AI Blueprint for 3D-guided generative AI, addresses this by integrating 3D scene drafts from Blender to guide image composition. This workflow uses depth maps to ensure accurate object placement and camera angles, eliminating the need for overly detailed text prompts.

The Blueprint leverages ComfyUI, a tool that chains generative AI models, along with NVIDIA’s FLUX.1-dev model, optimized for RTX GPUs. By utilizing TensorRT acceleration and FP4/FP8 quantization, the system delivers faster inference speeds and reduced VRAM usage. This setup is designed for GeForce RTX 4080 GPUs or higher, ensuring high-performance execution for both AI artists and developers.

A Prebuilt Foundation for Generative AI Workflows

NVIDIA’s Blueprint provides a complete package, including Blender, ComfyUI, plug-ins, and deployment instructions, streamlining the setup for advanced image generation. The included documentation and sample assets help users quickly adapt the workflow to their needs. For developers, the open-source components offer a foundation to build or expand custom pipelines.

Real-Time Generation Powered by RTX AI

Optimized for Blackwell and Ada Lovelace GPUs, the FLUX.1-dev microservice doubles inference speeds compared to native PyTorch FP16. This efficiency enables rapid iteration, making high-quality AI-generated imagery more accessible.

Generative AI Transforms South Korea’s Webcomics Industry

Legendary South Korean cartoonist Lee Hyun-se is pioneering AI in webcomics, using Stable Diffusion to preserve his iconic characters like Kkachi. His AI-assisted remake of Karon’s Dawn marks a shift in digital storytelling, blending human artistry with machine-generated visuals.

Younger artists remain divided—some fear AI threatens artistic identity, while others see it as a tool for efficiency. Startups like Onoma AI are accelerating production with tools like TooToon, though copyright concerns persist.

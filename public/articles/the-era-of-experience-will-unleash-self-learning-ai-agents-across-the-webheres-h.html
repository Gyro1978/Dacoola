The ‘Era of Experience’ Will Unleash Self-Learning AI Agents Across the Web

AI experts foresee an "Era of Experience" where self-learning AI agents operate autonomously, reshaping how applications are designed and deployed. This shift will require developers and businesses to adapt to new paradigms in AI-driven interactions.

The rise of these agents could lead to more intuitive, adaptive systems that learn from user behavior in real time. Preparing for this evolution means rethinking traditional software architectures to accommodate dynamic, self-improving AI.

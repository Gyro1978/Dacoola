Don Diablo’s BLACKOUT Music Video Pushes Boundaries With NVIDIA RTX Generative AI

Electronic music pioneer Don Diablo has unveiled the groundbreaking music video for his latest single, BLACKOUT, created using a hybrid generative AI workflow powered by NVIDIA RTX technology. The video blends locally processed AI imagery with cloud-based animation, showcasing how artists can harness cutting-edge tools for high-fidelity creative control.

Hybrid AI Workflow Redefines Music Video Production

The BLACKOUT video merges an industrial warehouse setting with AI-generated environments, stylized effects, and personalized elements reflecting Diablo’s artistic identity. Key to the process was a split workflow: image creation was handled locally on an NVIDIA GeForce RTX 5090 GPU using ComfyUI, while final animation was rendered via Kling AI’s cloud platform. This approach allowed rapid iteration and precise visual tuning.

A custom facial model trained on Diablo’s likeness ensured consistency across frames, integrated into a Stable Diffusion pipeline for stylized generation. The team then leveraged Kling AI’s physics and cinematic tools to animate the static imagery into dynamic scenes. “AI lets us shape visuals in real time,” Diablo noted, emphasizing the technology’s role in creative storytelling.

NVIDIA RTX and the Future of AI-Driven Art

The project highlights how RTX-powered local AI tools can complement cloud platforms for demanding workloads. Artists gain autonomy over early-stage ideation while tapping scalable resources for final output. NVIDIA’s Studio platform and RTX AI PCs are increasingly central to such workflows, offering access to advanced models and optimized performance.

For creators exploring similar techniques, resources like the RTX AI Garage blog provide insights into building generative AI pipelines. As Diablo’s project demonstrates, the fusion of artistic vision and RTX acceleration is redefining what’s possible in digital storytelling.

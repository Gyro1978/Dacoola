Waymo Says It Will Add 2,000 More Robotaxis in 2026

Waymo has announced plans to expand its robotaxi fleet by adding 2,000 more Jaguar I-Pace SUVs in 2026, bringing its total fleet size to 3,500. The company currently operates 1,500 autonomous vehicles across San Francisco, Los Angeles, Phoenix, and Austin, averaging 250,000 paid trips per week. The new vehicles will be retrofitted with autonomous tech at Waymo’s Arizona factory, which also prepares for future models like the Hyundai Ioniq 5 and Zeekr RT minivan.

Why It Matters

Waymo’s fleet expansion signals confidence in its autonomous driving technology and growing demand for robotaxi services. The move comes as the company eyes new markets, including Atlanta, Miami, and Washington, DC, in 2026. Additionally, with Tesla entering the robotaxi space and Waymo’s partnership with Toyota, the race for autonomous vehicle dominance is heating up. Scaling production efficiently will be key to maintaining a competitive edge in this rapidly evolving industry.

Instagram still posing serious risks to children, campaigners say

Instagram has introduced "built-in protections" for young users, but researchers claim the platform continues to expose children to sexualized images and harmful stereotypes. Despite promises of improved safety, campaigners argue these measures remain insufficient in shielding minors from inappropriate content.

Why It Matters

Child safety on social media remains a critical issue, with platforms like Instagram under increasing scrutiny. The persistence of harmful content suggests gaps in enforcement and algorithmic moderation, raising concerns about long-term impacts on young users' mental health. This highlights the ongoing tension between platform self-regulation and the need for stricter oversight to protect vulnerable audiences.

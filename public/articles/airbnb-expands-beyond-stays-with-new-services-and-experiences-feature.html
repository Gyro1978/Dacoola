Airbnb Expands Beyond Stays With New Services and Experiences Feature

Airbnb has unveiled a significant feature update, enabling travelers to book services and experiences-from massages and chef-prepared meals to cultural tours and outdoor activities-directly through its platform. The update, part of a broader app redesign, allows these offerings to be booked independently or added to existing stays. Initially available in 100 cities across eight countries, the move positions Airbnb to compete more directly with platforms like Tripadvisor and Viator while capitalizing on its existing user base.

Key Innovations & Market Impact

The new feature marks Airbnb’s second attempt at integrating experiences, following a 2023 pause to refine its core stay offerings. Now, the company aims to tap into the 1.5 billion annual site visitors who often browse without booking accommodations. Services span 10 categories, including personal training, photography, and spa treatments, while experiences cover 19 categories like food tours and art workshops.

Airbnb’s VP of Product Marketing, Judson Coplan, emphasized the platform’s unique appeal: "These offerings let travelers experience a city like a local." The company is also launching Airbnb Originals, exclusive experiences with celebrity partnerships, such as pastry-making with chef Raphaelle Elbaz or beach volleyball with Olympian Carolina Solberg.

Social and AI Enhancements

The update includes upcoming social features, like group chats for experience participants, and an AI-powered concierge to streamline trip planning. CEO Brian Chesky highlighted the AI assistant’s evolution, now providing direct answers instead of redirecting to support articles.

Pros & Cons

Pros
  • **Diverse offerings**: Combines stays with services like massages and cooking classes, reducing the need for multiple platforms.
  • **Local immersion**: Exclusive *Airbnb Originals* provide unique, celebrity-led experiences.
Cons
  • **Limited availability**: Services and experiences are currently restricted to select cities.
  • **Higher fees**: Hosts face a 20% commission for experiences, which could deter some providers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I book Airbnb services without a stay?

Yes, services and experiences can be booked independently or added to an existing reservation.

How does Airbnb verify service providers?

Hosts undergo checks for experience, licenses, and online presence to ensure quality and safety.

When will Airbnb’s AI concierge be available globally?

The AI assistant will expand to more languages and countries later in 2025.

Microsoft expects some AI capacity constraints this quarter

Published on April 30, 2025 by AI News Team   |   View Original Source

Microsoft warns of AI capacity constraints amid surging demand

Microsoft executives cautioned during Wednesday’s earnings call that customers may face AI service disruptions due to overwhelming demand. EVP and CFO Amy Hood noted AI capacity constraints could emerge as early as June, as data center deployment lags behind growth.

The company is racing to expand infrastructure but faces challenges in scaling quickly enough. These constraints highlight the explosive demand for AI services across industries.

OpenAI Releases New Simulated Reasoning Models with Full Tool Access

OpenAI has introduced two advanced OpenAI reasoning models, o3 and o4-mini, which integrate simulated reasoning with comprehensive tool access, including web browsing, coding, visual analysis, and image generation. These models are the first from OpenAI to allow simultaneous use of all ChatGPT tools, marking a significant step forward in AI functionality. The release follows the earlier announcement of o3 in December, with o4-mini now replacing its predecessors, o1 and o3-mini.

Access to the new models begins today for ChatGPT Plus, Pro, and Team users, while Enterprise and Edu customers will gain entry next week. Free-tier users can experiment with o4-mini by selecting the "Think" option before submitting queries. OpenAI CEO Sam Altman confirmed that o3-pro will be available for Pro users in the coming weeks, signaling continued expansion of the reasoning-focused AI lineup.

The rollout underscores OpenAI’s commitment to refining AI reasoning capabilities while broadening accessibility. By enabling full tool integration, these models aim to enhance productivity and creative applications for diverse user tiers.

Amazon updates Q Business to let companies build public-facing chatbots

Published on April 30, 2025 by AI News Team   |   View Original Source
<h2>Amazon Q Business Enables Public-Facing Chatbots</h2> <p>Amazon has updated its Q Business AI assistant to support anonymous user access, allowing companies to build public-facing chatbots. The AWS-hosted tool can now be deployed on websites and support portals to answer questions, provide summaries, and complete tasks. </p> <p>This enhancement broadens Q Business’s utility beyond internal enterprise use, offering businesses a new way to engage customers. The move aligns with Amazon’s push to expand its AI offerings in competitive markets.</p>

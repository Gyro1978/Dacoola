Google’s new ‘Simplify’ feature for iOS uses AI to make dense text easier to understand

Published on May 06, 2025 by AI News Team   |   View Original Source
<h2>Google’s new ‘Simplify’ feature for iOS uses AI to make dense text easier to understand</h2> <p>Google’s iOS app is introducing a new <strong>Google Simplify feature</strong> that leverages AI to simplify complex or technical web content without requiring users to leave the page. Developed by Google Research, the tool uses Gemini to distill complicated text into more digestible formats while retaining key details. </p> <h3>Why It Matters</h3> <p>This feature could significantly improve accessibility for users who struggle with dense or jargon-heavy content, such as academic papers, legal documents, or technical guides. By integrating AI-powered simplification directly into the browsing experience, Google is streamlining comprehension without sacrificing accuracy—a step toward more inclusive digital content consumption. As AI-assisted reading tools become mainstream, this could set a precedent for how tech companies enhance user engagement with complex information.</p>

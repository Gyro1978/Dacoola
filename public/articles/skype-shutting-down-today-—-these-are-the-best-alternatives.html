Skype shutting down today — these are the best alternatives

After 23 years of operation, Skype, the widely used video-calling service, is officially shutting down today, May 5, 2025. Microsoft, its parent company, confirmed the shutdown, urging users to migrate their contacts and chat data to Microsoft Teams or explore other alternatives.

Why It Matters

The Skype shutdown marks the end of an era for one of the pioneers in digital communication. Its discontinuation reflects Microsoft’s strategic shift toward Teams, which has become its flagship collaboration tool. For long-time Skype users, this transition may require adapting to new platforms, highlighting the evolving nature of tech services and the importance of staying updated with industry shifts.

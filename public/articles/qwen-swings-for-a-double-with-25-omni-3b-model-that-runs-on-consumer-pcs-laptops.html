Qwen2.5-Omni-3B AI Model Release Targets Consumer PCs

Alibaba Cloud has unveiled the Qwen2.5-Omni-3B AI model, designed to run efficiently on consumer PCs and laptops. The model is available under the Qwen Research License Agreement, restricting it to non-commercial use.

Pinterest Takes Action Against AI-Generated Image Overload

Pinterest is implementing new measures to tackle the surge of low-quality AI-generated content on its platform. The company announced plans to label AI-modified images and introduce user controls to reduce such content in feeds.

New Tools for Transparency

The platform will now clearly mark AI-generated or altered images, addressing growing user concerns. Additionally, Pinterest will offer a setting to limit AI content visibility, giving users more control over their browsing experience.

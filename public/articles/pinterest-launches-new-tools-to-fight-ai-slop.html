Pinterest launches new tools to fight AI slop

Published on April 30, 2025 by AI News Team   |   View Original Source
<h2>Pinterest Takes Action Against AI-Generated Image Overload</h2> <p>Pinterest is implementing new measures to tackle the surge of low-quality AI-generated content on its platform. The company announced plans to label AI-modified images and introduce user controls to reduce such content in feeds. </p> <h3>New Tools for Transparency</h3> <p>The platform will now clearly mark AI-generated or altered images, addressing growing user concerns. Additionally, Pinterest will offer a setting to limit AI content visibility, giving users more control over their browsing experience.</p>

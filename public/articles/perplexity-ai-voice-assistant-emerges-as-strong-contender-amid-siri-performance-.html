Perplexity AI Voice Assistant Emerges as Strong Contender Amid Siri Performance Issues

As Siri continues to struggle with basic tasks and Apple delays its "smarter Siri" update until late 2025, Perplexity AI's Voice Assistant is stepping in to fill the gap. Offering advanced conversational abilities, seamless web integration, and better contextual understanding, Perplexity is positioning itself as a viable alternative for users frustrated with Siri performance issues.

How Perplexity Outperforms Siri

Perplexity's Voice Assistant isn't just another AI chatbot-it's a fully functional voice assistant capable of handling tasks Siri still struggles with. From creating detailed calendar events to drafting coherent emails, Perplexity demonstrates a level of contextual awareness that Siri lacks. Unlike Siri, which often requires follow-up prompts, Perplexity maintains smooth, continuous conversations, remembering details and anticipating user needs.

One standout feature is its ability to perform web-based actions. Ask Perplexity to book a restaurant reservation, and it populates OpenTable with the details. Request an Uber, and it opens the app with the destination pre-filled. These are functionalities Apple Intelligence promises but won’t deliver until late 2025.

Key Features of Perplexity Voice Assistant

  • Background Mode: Unlike Siri, Perplexity remains active in the background, allowing uninterrupted conversations even while using other apps.
  • Apple App Integration: It syncs with Calendar, Reminders, and Mail, adding events and drafting emails with minimal user input.
  • Web-Connected Knowledge: Perplexity pulls real-time data from the web, answering questions Siri would defer to ChatGPT.

Pros & Cons

Pros
  • **Seamless multitasking** with background voice mode, unlike Siri’s one-off interactions.
  • **Better contextual understanding** for drafting emails, reminders, and event details.
  • **Web-enabled actions** like booking reservations or ride-hailing without switching apps.
Cons
  • **Limited OS-level access**-cannot toggle settings, send texts, or set alarms like Siri.
  • **Occasional hiccups** in messaging and contact integration, requiring manual inputs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can Perplexity Voice Assistant replace Siri completely?

No. While Perplexity excels in web-based tasks and conversation, it lacks deep iOS integration for system-level functions like alarms or messages.

How do I enable Perplexity Voice Assistant on iOS?

Download the Perplexity app, tap the waveform icon in the search bar, and start speaking. You can also add a widget for quick access.

Does Perplexity work with Apple Music and YouTube?

Yes. It plays music via Apple Music and can search and stream YouTube videos directly within the app.

AI Model Comparison: ChatGPT Outperforms Gemini, Perplexity, and Grok in Deep Research

AI chatbots are increasingly offering deep research features, allowing them to autonomously search the web, analyze sources, and compile detailed reports. This AI model comparison evaluates how ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Grok handle deep research tasks. Using a query about time travel in film and television, we tested each model's speed, depth, and accuracy.

Key Findings in AI Model Performance

ChatGPT emerged as the top performer, delivering the most thorough and well-structured report, though it took the longest-17 minutes for the full version. Google Gemini provided a scholarly but lengthy analysis in about 10 minutes, while Perplexity AI's results were disappointingly brief despite a quick turnaround. Surprisingly, Grok AI impressed with fast yet insightful reports, completing research in under three minutes.

Each model has distinct strengths. ChatGPT's full Deep Research mode is ideal for in-depth analysis, while Grok excels in speed without sacrificing quality. Gemini offers a balanced approach, though its formal tone may not appeal to all users. Perplexity, despite its high query limits, fell short in depth.

How Each AI Handles Deep Research

ChatGPT offers two research modes: full (detailed but slow) and lightweight (quicker but less comprehensive). Subscribers get varying query limits, with free users restricted to lightweight reports.

Gemini provides real-time updates during research, making its process transparent. However, its reports lean toward academic rigor, which may not suit casual readers.

Perplexity AI delivers fast results but lacks depth, making it better suited for quick overviews rather than detailed analysis.

Grok AI stands out for speed, with DeepSearch and DeeperSearch modes offering rapid yet surprisingly well-structured reports.

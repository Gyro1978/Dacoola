Meta Forecasts Massive AI Revenue Growth by 2035

Meta predicted last year that its generative AI products would bring in $2 billion to $3 billion in revenue by 2025, according to court documents unsealed Wednesday. The filings reveal even more ambitious long-term projections, estimating Meta AI revenue could reach between $460 billion and $1.4 trillion by 2035.

The forecasts emerged in legal documents submitted by attorneys representing authors suing Meta over alleged unauthorized use of copyrighted material to train AI models. While the projections highlight Meta's confidence in AI monetization, the ongoing lawsuit underscores legal challenges facing the industry.

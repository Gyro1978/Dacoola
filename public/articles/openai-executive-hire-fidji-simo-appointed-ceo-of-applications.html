OpenAI Executive Hire: Fidji Simo Appointed CEO of Applications

OpenAI has named Fidji Simo, former Instacart CEO and Meta executive, as its new CEO of Applications. The move, announced Wednesday, positions Simo to oversee business and operational teams while reporting directly to CEO Sam Altman. Simo, who joined OpenAI’s board in March 2024, will lead the company’s applications division, including products like ChatGPT.

The hiring comes as OpenAI enters its next phase of growth, with Altman describing the role as critical for managing "traditional company functions." The announcement was accelerated due to an internal leak, according to Altman.

Leadership Expansion at OpenAI

Simo brings a decade of experience from Meta, where she led Facebook from 2019 to 2021, along with her recent tenure at Instacart. Her appointment reflects OpenAI’s focus on scaling its operations while maintaining strong leadership. The newly created role suggests a strategic shift toward consolidating business functions under a dedicated executive.

OpenAI’s executive hire trends in 2025 highlight a pattern of recruiting seasoned tech leaders to bolster its management team. Simo’s background in social media and e-commerce-she also sits on Shopify’s board-positions her to drive OpenAI’s consumer-facing applications.

What This Means for OpenAI’s Future

Simo’s leadership could streamline decision-making as OpenAI expands its product lineup. With ChatGPT already a dominant AI assistant, her operational expertise may accelerate development and deployment of new tools. The hire also signals OpenAI’s intent to balance rapid innovation with structured corporate growth.

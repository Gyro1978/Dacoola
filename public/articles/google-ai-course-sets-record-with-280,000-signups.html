Google AI Course Sets Record with 280,000 Signups

Google and Kaggle’s GenAI Intensive course made history by attracting over 280,000 signups in just 20 days, earning a Guinness World Record for the largest virtual AI conference attendance in a week. The five-day Google AI course covered generative AI fundamentals, hands-on labs, and expert-led discussions.

Key Features of the GenAI Intensive Course

The course was structured to provide a mix of theory and practical learning. Each day focused on a different aspect of generative AI, from foundational models to domain-specific large language models (LLMs). Participants accessed AI-generated podcasts, whitepapers, and interactive labs on Kaggle, including tools like the Gemini API and Vertex AI.

A capstone project allowed learners to apply their knowledge, while a moderated Discord community and live Q&A sessions with Google experts enhanced engagement. For those who missed the live sessions, a self-paced version is now available on Kaggle.

Course Breakdown

  • Day 1: Covered foundational models and prompt engineering.
  • Day 2: Explored embeddings and vector databases.
  • Day 3: Focused on generative AI agents.
  • Day 4: Addressed domain-specific LLMs.
  • Day 5: Covered MLOps for generative AI.

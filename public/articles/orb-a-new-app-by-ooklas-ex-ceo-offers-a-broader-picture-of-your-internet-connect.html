Orb Expands Internet Testing Beyond Speed

Doug Suttles, founder and former CEO of Ookla, has launched Orb, a new internet connection app designed to provide a more comprehensive view of network performance. Unlike Speedtest.net, which focuses solely on speed, Orb evaluates reliability and other key metrics to give users a fuller understanding of their connectivity.

The app aims to address gaps in traditional speed tests by incorporating real-world usage data. Suttles believes this approach will help users better diagnose and optimize their internet experience.

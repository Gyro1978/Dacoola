Epic Games Scores Legal Victory Against Apple

Epic Games secured a win in its ongoing Apple legal dispute, with a judge ruling Apple violated a 2021 injunction against anticompetitive pricing. The decision could pave the way for Fortnite’s return to the U.S. iOS App Store as early as next week.

Judge Yvonne Gonzalez Rogers declared Apple in "willful violation" of the previous order, marking a significant setback for the tech giant. The ruling may force Apple to adjust its App Store policies to comply with antitrust regulations.

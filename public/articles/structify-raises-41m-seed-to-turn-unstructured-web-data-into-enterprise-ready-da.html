Structify Raises $4.1M to Transform Unstructured Data for AI

Brooklyn-based AI data startup Structify has emerged from stealth with $4.1 million in seed funding. The company aims to automate the preparation of unstructured web data for enterprise AI, a task that currently consumes 80% of data scientists' time.

Founded by Alex Reichenbach, Alex Goldstein, and Ronak Gandhi, Structify promises to turn raw web data into ready-to-use datasets. The funding will help scale its solution, reducing manual data processing burdens for businesses.

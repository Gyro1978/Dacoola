NVIDIA Brings Cybersecurity to Every AI Factory

As enterprises increasingly adopt AI, securing AI factories—where complex, agentic workflows are executed—has become a top priority. NVIDIA addresses this challenge with its new AI cybersecurity framework, DOCA Argus, part of the NVIDIA cybersecurity AI platform. Running on the NVIDIA BlueField networking platform, DOCA Argus provides runtime threat detection without impacting system performance, delivering speeds up to 1,000x faster than traditional agentless solutions.

Unlike conventional security tools, DOCA Argus operates independently of the host, requiring no agents or host-based resources. This agentless, zero-overhead design ensures resilient security in containerized and multi-tenant AI environments while remaining invisible to attackers—even during a system compromise. The framework integrates seamlessly with enterprise security systems like SIEM, SOAR, and XDR, enabling continuous monitoring and automated threat mitigation.

NVIDIA’s collaboration with Cisco further strengthens AI factory security by embedding protection at every infrastructure layer. The DOCA Argus framework leverages NVIDIA’s AI-driven insights to reduce false positives, providing actionable intelligence for security teams. With real-time threat detection and autonomous response capabilities, enterprises can confidently scale AI workloads while safeguarding critical data.

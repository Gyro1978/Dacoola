TeleMessage, a modified Signal clone used by US govt. officials, has been hacked

Published on May 05, 2025 by AI News Team   |   View Original Source
<h2>TeleMessage, a modified Signal clone used by US govt. officials, has been hacked</h2> <p>A hacker has breached TeleMessage, a service offering modified versions of encrypted messaging apps like Signal and WhatsApp, extracting archived messages and data tied to US government officials and companies. The exploit, reported by 404 Media, follows recent scrutiny of TeleMessage's security practices. </p> <h3>Why It Matters</h3> <p>This breach highlights the risks of relying on third-party modifications of secure messaging platforms, especially for sensitive government communications. The exposure of archived messages could compromise confidential discussions, raising concerns about data integrity and national security. The incident underscores the need for stricter oversight of customized encryption tools used by officials.</p>

Roblox Commerce APIs Enable Physical Product Sales in Games

Roblox has officially rolled out its Commerce APIs, allowing creators to sell physical merchandise directly within their games and experiences. This move opens a new revenue channel beyond virtual goods, integrating real-world shopping into the platform. Initially tested with select brands, the feature is now available to all eligible U.S. creators and users aged 13+.

The APIs, developed in partnership with Shopify, let creators bundle physical items with digital counterparts-like a hoodie for both your avatar and yourself. Brands like Fenty are already leveraging this, offering exclusive products like the "Grape Splash" Gloss Bomb shade via shoppable Roblox experiences.

Expanding Revenue and Shopping Experiences

Roblox’s Commerce APIs mark a strategic shift toward blending virtual and physical commerce. Creators in the closed beta, such as Twin Atlas, reported six-figure revenues within weeks, with 90% of orders coming through in-game integrations. The platform’s Approved Merchandiser Program (AMP) adds authenticity badges to physical products, which include codes for matching digital items.

The company envisions a future where shopping on Roblox is social and interactive-exploring virtual stores, trying on clothes with friends, and instantly purchasing real-world items. Currently limited to the U.S., Roblox plans to expand to more markets and partners soon.

How It Works for Creators and Brands

Eligible creators can connect their Shopify stores or physical inventories to Roblox, offering seamless in-game purchases. For brands, this means tapping into Roblox’s young, engaged audience. Fenty’s exclusive launch exemplifies how beauty and fashion brands can capitalize on the platform’s immersive environments.

Bundling is another key feature: buying a physical item unlocks a digital version, enhancing value for users. This dual offering could drive higher conversion rates, as seen in early beta results.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can use Roblox’s Commerce APIs?

Eligible creators and brands in the U.S. can now access the APIs, with plans to expand globally.

What’s the benefit of bundling physical and digital items?

Bundles increase perceived value, encouraging purchases. For example, buying a real hoodie also grants a digital version for your avatar.

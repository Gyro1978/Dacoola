UiPath’s Maestro Orchestrates AI Agents for Enterprise Compliance

UiPath has introduced Maestro, an AI agent orchestration layer designed to guide automation workflows across enterprises. The system processes prompts through three layers: the AI agent, human oversight, and the robotic process automation (RPA) system.

Maestro ensures AI agents adhere to organizational rules, improving efficiency and compliance. By integrating human input with automated processes, UiPath aims to enhance hyperautomation capabilities for businesses.

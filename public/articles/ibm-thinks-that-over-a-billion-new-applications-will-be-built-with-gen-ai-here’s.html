IBM Thinks That Over a Billion New Applications Will Be Built With Gen AI: Here’s How They’re Going to Help That Happen With Agentic AI

IBM is expanding its agentic AI capabilities to help enterprises unlock the potential of generative AI, aiming to facilitate the creation of over a billion new applications. The company is focusing on enhancing AI-driven automation and decision-making tools to empower businesses in scaling AI adoption effectively.

Why It Matters

IBM’s push into agentic AI highlights the growing demand for AI systems that can autonomously execute complex tasks, reducing reliance on manual intervention. By enabling enterprises to build and deploy AI-driven applications at scale, IBM is positioning itself as a key player in the next wave of enterprise AI adoption. This move could accelerate innovation across industries, from healthcare to finance, by making AI more accessible and actionable for businesses.

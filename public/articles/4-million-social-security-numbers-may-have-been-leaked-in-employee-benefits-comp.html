4 Million Social Security Numbers May Have Been Leaked in Employee Benefits Company Breach

A significant data breach at VeriSource Services, an employee benefits company, may have exposed the Social Security numbers of up to 4 million individuals. The incident raises concerns about identity theft, and affected users are urged to take protective measures.

Why It Matters

This breach highlights the growing risks of sensitive personal data exposure, especially when involving Social Security numbers—a key target for fraudsters. Given VeriSource’s role in employee benefits, the leak could impact individuals across multiple organizations, amplifying the potential for financial harm. The incident underscores the need for stronger cybersecurity measures in third-party service providers handling critical personal data.

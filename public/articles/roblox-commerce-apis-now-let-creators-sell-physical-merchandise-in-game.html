Roblox Commerce APIs Now Let Creators Sell Physical Merchandise In-Game

Published on May 15, 2025 by Gyro Pick Team   |   View Original Source
<h2>Roblox Commerce APIs Now Let Creators Sell Physical Merchandise In-Game</h2> <p>Roblox has officially launched its Commerce APIs, allowing creators to sell physical merchandise directly within their games and experiences. This move expands revenue opportunities beyond virtual goods, enabling U.S. users aged 13 and up to purchase real-world products like apparel and accessories while playing. The feature, tested earlier with select brands, is now widely available after a closed beta, with plans for global expansion. </p> <!-- DACCOOLA_IN_ARTICLE_AD_HERE --> <h3>A New Revenue Stream for Creators and Brands</h3> <p>The Commerce APIs integrate with Shopify, letting creators link physical stores to their Roblox experiences. For example, beauty brand Fenty will debut an exclusive "Grape Splash" Gloss Bomb shade via a shoppable in-game store. Creators can also bundle physical items with digital counterparts-buying a hoodie in real life could unlock one for your avatar. </p> <p>Roblox’s Approved Merchandiser Program (AMP) adds authenticity badges to physical products, which include codes for exclusive digital items. Early adopters like Twin Atlas reported six-figure revenue within weeks, with 90% of orders coming through in-game integrations. </p> <h4>Bridging Virtual and Physical Shopping</h4> <p>Roblox envisions a social shopping future where users explore virtual stores, try on items, and share the experience with friends. "This builds purchase intent naturally," the company stated, emphasizing the seamless transition from digital browsing to real-world buying. Partnerships with additional brands and regional expansions are expected soon. </p> <h4>Frequently Asked Questions</h4> <div class="faq-section"> <details class="faq-item"> <summary class="faq-question">Who can use Roblox’s Commerce APIs? <i class="faq-icon fas fa-chevron-down"></i></summary> <div class="faq-answer-content"> <p>Eligible creators and brands in the U.S. can now access the APIs, with plans to expand globally.</p> </div> </details> <details class="faq-item"> <summary class="faq-question">Can physical purchases include digital items? <i class="faq-question"> <div class="faq-answer-content"> <p>Yes, the APIs support bundling-like receiving a digital avatar hoodie with a physical purchase.</p> </div> </details> </div>

Films made with AI can win Oscars, Academy says

The Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences has clarified that films created with AI in Oscars consideration are eligible to win awards, though human involvement will still be a key factor in selecting winners. This decision acknowledges the growing role of AI in filmmaking while maintaining the importance of human creativity.

Why It Matters

This ruling marks a significant moment for the entertainment industry as AI tools become more sophisticated in generating scripts, visuals, and even performances. While the Academy is open to recognizing AI-assisted films, its emphasis on human input suggests a balancing act between innovation and artistic tradition.

Microsoft Build Conference 2025: How to Watch Live

Microsoft Build, the company’s flagship annual event, kicks off on May 19 at the Seattle Convention Center, running through May 22. The keynote, featuring CEO Satya Nadella and CTO Kevin Scott, begins at 9:05 a.m. PT on the first day. A follow-up session with executives Charles Lamanna and Scott Guthrie will take place May 20 at 9 a.m. PT. Both presentations will be streamed live for free.

Keynote and AI Focus

The Microsoft Build conference is expected to highlight the company’s AI-driven Copilot products, reflecting its aggressive push to integrate AI across its software and services. Recent moves, such as bundling Copilot into Microsoft 365 subscriptions and raising prices by 30%, signal Microsoft’s intent to accelerate AI monetization.

In recent weeks, Microsoft has expanded Copilot’s capabilities, adding web browsing and task automation features. Reports also suggest the company is intensifying internal AI research to reduce reliance on OpenAI’s models, despite their current partnership.

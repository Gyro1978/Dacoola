AI in Healthcare: Computing the Uncomputable

The University of Texas Medical Branch is leveraging AI in healthcare to tackle previously uncomputable challenges, such as identifying high-risk cardiovascular patients and flagging potential stroke cases. The technology also helps catch overlooked "basic stuff," streamlining diagnostics and improving patient outcomes.

Transforming Patient Care

By analyzing vast datasets, AI enables faster, more accurate risk assessments, allowing healthcare providers to intervene earlier. This breakthrough marks a significant step toward overcoming the "intellectual bottleneck" in medical decision-making.

Startups Pioneer Crowd-Trained LLMs Without Data Centers

A new wave of startups is developing large language models (LLMs) through crowd-training over the internet, bypassing traditional data centers. The approach could lead to a groundbreaking 100 billion-parameter model by late 2025, challenging conventional AI development.

This decentralized method leverages untapped computing resources globally, reducing infrastructure costs. Experts suggest crowd-trained LLMs may democratize AI innovation while accelerating model scalability.

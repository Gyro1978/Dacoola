Threads Surpasses 350 Million Monthly Active Users

Instagram Threads, Meta’s competitor to X, has grown to over 350 million monthly active users, CEO Mark Zuckerberg announced during the Q1 2025 earnings call. The platform added 30 million users this quarter, up from 320 million in the previous period, signaling accelerating Threads user growth.

The latest figures highlight Threads’ expanding reach as Meta continues to invest in the social media platform. While details on engagement metrics weren’t disclosed, the steady rise suggests increasing adoption among users.

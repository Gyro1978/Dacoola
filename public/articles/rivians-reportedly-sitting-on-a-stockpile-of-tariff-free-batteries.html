Rivian Amassed Tariff-Free Battery Stockpile

Rivian reportedly built a significant stockpile of lithium iron phosphate batteries from Chinese supplier Gotion before and after Donald Trump's election. The Rivian battery stockpile strategy aimed to offset potential tariff hikes under the Trump administration, according to Bloomberg News.

The company secured an undisclosed number of batteries for its trucks, SUVs, and commercial vans. This preemptive move could help Rivian maintain cost efficiency amid fluctuating trade policies.

Duolingo Debuts 148 AI-Generated Language Courses

Duolingo has introduced 148 new language courses created using generative AI, doubling its course offerings. The move comes as the company faces backlash for its shift toward becoming an "AI-first" organization, including plans to replace contractors with AI tools.

The expansion highlights the growing role of AI in education, though it has sparked debate about job displacement in the language-learning sector. Duolingo maintains that AI integration enhances scalability and accessibility for learners worldwide.

Duolingo launches 148 courses created with AI after sharing plans to replace contractors with AI

Published on April 30, 2025 by AI News Team   |   View Original Source
<h2>Duolingo Debuts 148 AI-Generated Language Courses</h2> <p>Duolingo has introduced 148 new language courses created using generative AI, doubling its course offerings. The move comes as the company faces backlash for its shift toward becoming an "AI-first" organization, including plans to replace contractors with AI tools. </p> <p>The expansion highlights the growing role of AI in education, though it has sparked debate about job displacement in the language-learning sector. Duolingo maintains that AI integration enhances scalability and accessibility for learners worldwide.</p>

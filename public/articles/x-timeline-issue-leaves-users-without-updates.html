X Timeline Issue Leaves Users Without Updates

Many X users reported their timelines stopped updating late Thursday night, with notifications also failing to work. The issue appears to be a server-side bug affecting both mobile apps and the web version, persisting for over 15 hours in some cases. Attempts to resolve it by switching devices, browsers, or subscribing to X Premium have been unsuccessful.

Widespread Reports of Disruption

Downdetector recorded a surge in X outage reports over the past 24 hours, while Reddit threads filled with complaints from users worldwide. One German user noted missing notifications since 10 p.m., while another tried reinstalling the app without success. X has not yet commented on the cause or a potential fix.

This isn’t the first time X has faced major outages. In March, Musk blamed a cyberattack for a global disruption, though no evidence was provided. Similar issues occurred in December 2022 and July 2023, raising concerns about platform stability following Musk’s acquisition and workforce reductions.

Previous Outages and Staff Cuts

Since Elon Musk’s $44 billion takeover in 2022, X has seen multiple rounds of layoffs, cutting its workforce by 80%. By January 2023, only 550 full-time engineers remained, according to CNBC. Another wave of layoffs in November 2024 further impacted the engineering team, potentially contributing to ongoing technical challenges.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the X timeline issue affecting all users?

No, the problem appears sporadic, with some users reporting normal functionality while others experience prolonged delays.

Has X acknowledged the problem?

As of now, X has not issued an official statement regarding the timeline issue.

Are there any temporary fixes?

Switching devices or browsers hasn’t resolved the issue, suggesting it’s server-related rather than client-side.

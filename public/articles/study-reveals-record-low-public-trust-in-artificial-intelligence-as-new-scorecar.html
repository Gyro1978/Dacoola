Public Trust in AI Reaches Historic Low

A recent study reveals that Public Trust in AI has plummeted to an all-time low, according to findings published by ABN Newswire. The report coincides with the introduction of a new AI scorecard system designed to assess AI's societal and ethical implications.

AI Scorecard Introduced Amid Skepticism

The scorecard aims to provide transparency by evaluating AI technologies on factors like fairness, accountability, and bias. Experts suggest this tool could help rebuild confidence as concerns over AI misuse grow.

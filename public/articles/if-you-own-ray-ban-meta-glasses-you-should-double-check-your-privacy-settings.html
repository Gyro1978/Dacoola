Meta Updates AI Privacy Policy for Ray-Ban Glasses

Meta has revised its AI privacy policy for Ray-Ban Meta smart glasses, expanding its authority over stored data and AI model training. Owners received an email Tuesday notifying them that AI features will now be enabled by default.

The change means users must manually adjust settings if they wish to limit data collection. Meta's move aligns with broader efforts to enhance AI capabilities through user interactions.

Nuvo Raises $34M to Expand B2B Trade Network

Nuvo, a San Francisco-based startup, has secured $34 million in Series A funding led by Sequoia Capital and Spark Capital. The company’s social-like platform simplifies B2B trade funding by facilitating easier purchasing of physical goods between businesses.

Previously, Nuvo raised $11 million in a seed round backed by Founders Fund and Index Ventures. The latest investment will likely accelerate its mission to modernize B2B transactions.

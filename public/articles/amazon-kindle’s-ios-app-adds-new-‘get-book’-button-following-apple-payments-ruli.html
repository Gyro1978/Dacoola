Amazon Kindle’s iOS app adds new ‘Get Book’ button following Apple payments ruling

Amazon’s Kindle iOS app has introduced a new “Get book” button, making it easier for users to purchase titles directly. This change follows a recent ruling that barred Apple from charging a 27% commission on in-app purchases. Amazon stated that the update is part of its ongoing efforts to improve customer convenience.

Why It Matters

This move highlights the broader impact of regulatory decisions on app ecosystems. By bypassing Apple’s fees, Amazon can offer a smoother purchasing experience while potentially lowering costs for consumers. It also signals a shift in how major platforms may adapt to antitrust rulings, reshaping digital marketplaces.
